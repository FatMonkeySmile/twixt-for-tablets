TwixT is a two-player abstract game invented by Alex Randolph. Each player tries to link their sides of the board while blocking their opponent. This is done by placing pegs which create links with other pegs if they are a "knights move" apart and not blocked by an opposing link. A knights move is two spaces either up, down, left or right, then one space perpendicular as shown in the image above. For more information about the game visit the TwixT entry on Wikipedia.
